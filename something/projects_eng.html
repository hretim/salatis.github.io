Homigrad



- Mode in the game Garry's mod | Mafia with realistic PVP
You mean "realistic" PvP?
Straight up, hit in the head but no brain damage, there's a chance of survival. Bone damage, organ damage, bleeding, et cetera. The most important feature of the server, was in the mode fake ( ragdoll ) Literally every player could take and fall off his feet, in order to, for example, crawl somewhere or so on. Also in the fake mode you could calmly shoot with their weapons, and even get a chance to survive.

Currently in the public domain for all to see, you can see it on my Git Hub.
Developed during the period: Early summer to early September 2023. It was the very first testbed for learning lua and glua, currently unsupported.

Was quite successful, but due to a conflict, the original server was shut down.
